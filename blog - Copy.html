There are dreamers and there are realists in this world. You’d think the dreamers would find the dreamers and the realists would find the realists, but more often than not the opposite is true. You see, the dreamers need the realists to keep them from soaring too close to the sun. And the realists, well, without the dreamers, they might not ever get off the ground.” (This was from an episode of Modern Family.)

Anyone who knows me, knows I have a strong affection for quotes about living our best life and inspirational messages. Words that inspire us to unite and celebrate our place in this world have always resonated with me; I am always listening for a nugget of wisdom in songs, movies and conversations. Lately, my radar has been attuned to quotes that remind us to be unified.

It is no secret that our society is in the midst of a great divide. I don't like it. It seems in the media, especially social media, we are encouraged to separate into groups or subcategories of human nature; to associate only with like minded people. There is a constant need for factions or groups to prove they are better than other groups. It is destroying our relationships, families and growth.

The truth is we need each other. We can learn much from those who are different than we are. Combining our strengths is a wonderful way to ensure we all succeed. Together, we can create all sorts of magic. Those are the stories I want to tell.
