SHEROES United Focuses on 3 KEY Pillars: Domestic Violence; Human Trafficking; and Women Warriors. SHEROES United is all about embracing and loving Women and the Men that love them. They do not want to go out and fight against violence or take any kind of a stand against Crime. They are a landing place for those that need and want to have a place to talk with like-minded people that understand and want to hear their stories.

Best Friends pioneers the no-kill movement. 30 years ago, more than 17 million homeless pets were dying each year in our nation's shelters. By implementing spay/neuter & trap/neuter/return (TNR) programs to reduce the number of animals entering shelters, & increasing the number of people who adopt pets, we've reduced that number to around four million deaths annually.

NKLA is an initiative led by Best Friends Animal Society, bringing together passionate individuals, city shelters and an entire coalition of animal welfare organizations to end the killing of healthy and treatable pets in city shelters in Los Angeles, California.

Gobble Gobble Give has become an incredible example of what a community can do to make change. If you are reading this, you have been invited to join us! Last year we were able to feed over 3,000 homeless in our neighborhood and leave them with t-shirts, beanies, shoes, sweaters, and tooth brush kits.

Hand to Hand Ministry provides a hot sit down meal every Saturday and most Holidays. Come join us as we nourish our guests with love and compassion! The quality of life for those who live on the streets will be raised up through your service.

We partner with private nonprofit organizations that raise money to carry out their mission to save the lives of our most vulnerable animals. The New Hope Program was implemented to eliminate obstacles that might prevent saving the lives of the animals most at risk of euthanasia due to space or treatable conditions.

The Giving Spirit provides aid to these vulnerable and fragile lives by assembling and personally distributing survival kits to the children, women and families living on the streets of Los Angeles. These kits contain over 70 individual items providing sustenance to the homeless in their time of need. Our efforts have impacted lives from Skid Row to Santa Monica.

Broadway Barks, the star-studded dog and cat adoption event benefiting New York City animal shelters and adoption agencies, will return for a 15th year this summer. The event, produced by Broadway Cares/Equity Fights AIDS, will again take place in Shubert Alley with festivities and celebrity presentations of pets from citywide animal shelters.
